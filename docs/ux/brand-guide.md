# Brand Guide

## Logo & Colors

- Primary: #2563eb (blue-600), #3b82f6 (blue-500)
- Secondary: #1d4ed8 (blue-700), #1e40af (blue-800)
- Text: White, Gray-300, Slate-400
- Backgrounds: Dark gradients with glassmorphic effects (rgba white overlays)

## Typography

- Display/Logo: Girassol (cursive)
- Headings: Inter, bold
- Body: Inter, regular

## Imagery

- Use student, tech, and AI-related imagery
- Showcase diversity and collaboration

## Voice & Tone

- Friendly, supportive, empowering
- Clear, actionable, inclusive
