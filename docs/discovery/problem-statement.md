# Problem Statement & Project Goals

## Problem Statement

Many NJIT students interested in AI lack a clear, guided pathway to build professional skills, assets, and networks needed for AI careers. They are often overwhelmed by resources, unsure how to start, and miss out on key opportunities for growth and connection.

## Project Goals

- Provide a structured onboarding and career pipeline for students interested in AI.
- Connect students to events, mentors, and resources.
- Guide students in building essential professional assets (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio, Calendly).
- Automate administrative workflows and communications.
- Track progress and engagement in a CRM.
- Ensure accessibility, SEO, analytics, and GDPR compliance.
