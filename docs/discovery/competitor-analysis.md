# Competitor/Comparable Analysis

## 1. Replit Community

- **Strengths:** Active forums, project sharing, mentorship, events.
- **Weaknesses:** Not AI-specific, less focus on professional onboarding.

## 2. Major League Hacking (MLH)

- **Strengths:** Hackathons, workshops, career resources, strong network.
- **Weaknesses:** Event-based, less ongoing support, not NJIT-specific.

## 3. University Career Centers

- **Strengths:** Professional guidance, resume/portfolio help, employer connections.
- **Weaknesses:** Less tech/AI focus, less peer community, slower to adapt to new tech.
